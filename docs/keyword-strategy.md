# Keyword Strategy: gregkowalczyk.com

## Business Context
- **Offer:** AI implementation consulting — custom tool development, marketing automation, e-commerce systems
- **Audience:** Small/mid business owners in Ontario (Burlington, Oakville, GTA, Toronto) looking to implement AI without hiring agencies
- **Goal:** Lead generation via contact form
- **Site type:** Single-page consulting site (v1), blog expansion in v2
- **Competitive edge:** Builder not consultant, engineer background, runs own mid-7-figure brands

## Top Opportunities

1. **"AI consultant Burlington" / "AI consultant Oakville"** — Near-zero competition for local intent keywords. Greg would be defining the category locally. Quick win for Google Business Profile + local search.
2. **"AI implementation consulting Ontario"** — Medium competition, high commercial intent. Few Ontario-based competitors actually build custom tools. Greg's mechanism differentiates.
3. **"Vibe coding consultant"** — First-mover advantage. Emerging term with almost no competitors. Greg's background (built 2 iOS apps with vibe coding tools) makes this authentic.
4. **FAQ section targeting People Also Ask** — "How much does AI implementation cost?" and similar PAA queries have low competition and high LLM citation potential.

## Quick Wins (1-3 months with site live)
- "AI consultant Burlington Ontario" — Local, near-zero competition
- "AI consultant Oakville" — Same, adjacent city
- "Vibe coding consultant" — First-mover, no competition
- "Replace agency costs with AI" — Long-tail, matches Greg's exact story
- "Custom AI tools for small business" — Specific, lower competition than broad terms

## Long-Term Plays (6-12 months)
- "AI consulting Toronto" — Higher competition, needs authority building
- "AI implementation consulting" (broad) — National term, needs backlinks
- "Marketing automation consulting" — Crowded, but Greg's builder angle differentiates
